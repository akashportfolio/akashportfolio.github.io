YouPlayFm

YouPlayFm is a music player that was created by myself along with a few friends. This website was made in just 24 hours! This music player is unique in the way it gathers musics. In contrast to Spotify and other big name music players, YouPlay gets its music from the endless source YouTube. It allows users to listen to remixes that they would not get from anywhere else. It also has mood feature for when you want to be spontaneous!

YouPlay was made at CodeDay NY and received a honorable mention.

  • Languages Used: HTML, CSS ,  Javasript
  • Date: May 2014
  • Service: Music Player

Clubbed In

Clubbed In is created by high schoolers, for high schoolers. Clubbed In is an app that encourages communication between the members of school clubs and is also a useful tool for club leaders to organize information. Mainly, the goal of the app is to to keep students active members of clubs. This application includes many features such as events, news, email notifications, attendance, and much more!

It can be downloaded on the Google Play Store or Apple Marketplace

  • Languages Used: HTML, CSS ,  Javasript
  • Date: April 2013
  • Service: LifeStyle Application

Tap App

Tap is an app that allows you to control your phone via a tap. This tap will allow you to skip to the next song by simply tapping your phone while in your pocket. It also switches active apps on your phone so the multi-tasking is redefined. It has many more features. You can download it here on the Google Play Store.

Tap was created at the 2014 spring HackRU hackathon. It placed Top 10 amongst over 60 applications. It was my group's very first hackathon and we are very proud of the outcome.

  • Languages Used: HTML, CSS ,  Javasript
  • Date: April 2014
  • Service: LifeStyle Application
